How to read this screen
Tier 1 ±2%Price is on the gate — sitting within 2% of the 10-month or 20-month SMA. A decision point: either breaks through and starts recovering, or gets rejected back down.
Tier 2 ±5%In the zone — price approaching the monthly MA gate. Watch for it to drift into Tier 1 as the next stage.
10mSMA43-week simple moving average. First recovery gate. Reclaiming this is the initial sign that structure is rebuilding.
20mSMA87-week simple moving average. Long-term floor. Strong support when price is below 10m. Can also act as ceiling on the way back up.
◎ vs →◎ = price is closer to 10mSMA · → = price is closer to 20mSMA. Tells you which gate is the immediate test.
vs 10m / vs 20mGreen = price above that SMA. Red = price below. A name can be above one and below the other — that's the gate zone.
When to useRun this screen just before or early in a recovery. These names are at the inflection — the monthly MA is either about to act as a springboard or as a ceiling.
No stack needed10m and 20m don't need to be stacked. This screen is about proximity to the gate, not full alignment. Full alignment shows up in the aligned screener once price reclaims both.
